Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 2026  ·  NaylorMade LLC

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and settings, to make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

How We Use Cookies

naylormade.io uses a minimal number of cookies to provide a functional and personalized experience:

  • Preference cookies — We store your dark/light mode preference locally in your browser (via localStorage) so it persists between visits. No data is sent to our servers.
  • Analytics cookies — If analytics tools are enabled on the Site, they may set cookies to help us understand how visitors interact with our content. Analytics data is aggregated and not used to identify individuals.

Essential vs. Non-Essential Cookies

The theme preference stored by this Site is considered a functional cookie essential to your user experience and does not require consent under most jurisdictions. Any analytics cookies are non-essential and are only set with your consent where required by applicable law.

Third-Party Cookies

The Site may link to or embed content from third-party services (such as LinkedIn or X). These services may set their own cookies when you interact with their content. We do not control third-party cookies and encourage you to review the respective privacy and cookie policies of those services.

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the Site. For more information on managing cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above will reflect any changes.
